RATING SCALE DEFINITIONS

Title Endpoints Descriptions
MENTAL DEMAND Low - High The amount of mental and perceptual activity required.
PHYSICAL DEMAND Low - High The amount of physical activity required.
TEMPORAL DEMAND Low - High The feeling of time pressure experienced.
PERFORMANCE Good - Poor The success in performing the required task.
EFFORT Low - High The level of effort required to accomplish the task.
FRUSTRATION Low - High The level of stress, irritation, or annoyance felt.
